Overview

Rooting Change, One Tree at a Time

Project Vriksh (meaning "Tree" in Sanskrit) is our environmental conservation initiative that goes beyond just planting trees. We focus on creating a sustainable ecosystem of environmental awareness, waste management, and community-driven green spaces.

From plantation drives in urban wastelands to educating children about climate change, Vriksh is building a generation of environmental stewards who understand the value of our planet.
