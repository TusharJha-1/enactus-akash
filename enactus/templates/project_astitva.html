Overview

Celebrating Every Woman's Identity

Astitva - meaning "identity" in Hindi - is our flagship women empowerment initiative. We believe every woman has a unique story worth telling and skills worth nurturing.

Through skill development workshops, financial literacy programs, and our award-winning magazine platform, we create spaces where women can discover their potential and share their journeys with the world.
