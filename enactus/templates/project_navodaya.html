Overview

Lighting the Path to Self-Reliance

Project Navodaya was born from a simple yet powerful idea: every young person deserves the opportunity to build a sustainable livelihood, regardless of their socioeconomic background.

We work with underprivileged youth from urban slums and rural areas, providing them with vocational training, entrepreneurship skills, and mentorship to help them become self-reliant contributors to society.

Vocational Training

Hands-on skills in tailoring, computer literacy, and mobile repair

Entrepreneurship Bootcamps

Business fundamentals, financial literacy, and startup guidance
